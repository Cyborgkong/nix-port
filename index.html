FAQ

Common Questions

Everything you need to know about working with a virtual assistant.

How many hours do you typically work with clients?

I offer flexible packages ranging from 10 to 40 hours per month, depending on your needs. We can start with a smaller package and adjust as we identify your specific requirements. Many clients begin with 15-20 hours monthly and find that provides significant relief from administrative burdens.

What if I need help with something not listed in your services?

My services list covers my core offerings, but I'm adaptable and experienced in many administrative areas. During our initial consultation, we can discuss your specific needs, and I'll let you know if I can help or recommend a specialist if needed. I'm continuously expanding my skillset to better serve my clients.

How do we communicate when working together?

I adapt to your preferred communication methods. Most clients use a combination of email, messaging platforms (like Slack), and regular video check-ins. During onboarding, we'll establish the communication protocols that work best for your workflow and preferences. I'm available during standard business hours and provide response time expectations.

What's your availability throughout the week?

I'm available Monday through Friday, 9am to 5pm Eastern Time. However, I can be flexible for urgent matters or to accommodate clients in different time zones. We'll discuss your specific needs during our consultation to ensure my availability aligns with your requirements.

How do you handle confidential information?

Confidentiality is paramount in my business. I sign a comprehensive non-disclosure agreement with all clients and use secure, encrypted tools for sharing and storing information. I follow strict protocols for data protection and maintain the highest ethical standards when handling sensitive information.

What's your pricing structure?

I offer tiered monthly retainer packages based on the scope and volume of support needed. Packages typically range from $500-$2,000 per month, depending on hours and complexity. I also offer project-based pricing for specific, one-time needs. During our consultation, I'll provide a custom quote based on your unique requirements.
